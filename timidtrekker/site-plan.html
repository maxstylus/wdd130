Overview

Purpose

Every homeschooling parent knows that homeschooling has very little to do with schooling at home. We know that the best education does not happen at a desk at all, but rather as we engage in everyday living, in exploring and studying the world around us and as we engage in our communities, develop relationships and experience everyday living. Despite knowing this, too many homeschooling parents get stuck at home while schooling. This is often because parents have forgotten the value and joy of learning through our environment and through experiences and aren’t informed of the many options and opportunities that exist around them.

Audience

This is a website that will serve as a “friend” or a resource to connect homeschoolers with local activities and opportunities that will bring joy and play back into the homeschoolers educational journey.

Home page - Adventures for Timid Trekkers

Your homeschool is not at home.

Every homeschooling parent knows that homeschooling has very little to do with schooling at home. We know that the best education does not happen at a desk at all, but rather as we engage in everyday living, in exploring and studying the world around us and as we engage in our communities, develop relationships and experience everyday living.

Ditch the Workbooks

The Timid Trekker works hard for you to collect opportunities for homeschooling parents to teach their children the love of learning. We aim to inspire you to get your child outdoors, learning in your community more and freed from endless workbooks and worksheets.

Come out and Play

Learning should be joyful. We learn most when we are in a state of play. Yet again and again we teach our children in the most tedious and laborious ways possible. All activities can be simply enjoyed or you can use some of our resources to incorporate some other educational topics such math, writing, art and music.

Homeschoolers can be Timid

We hope that as you and your homeschooler venture out into your community that you will learn to love many aspects of your community as you participate in the activities and adventures in your neighborhood and as you create opportunities to serve, learn and go with your friends and neighbors

Activities in Your Community

Never underestimate the learning power of local activities. All local activities can be expericienced with or without supplemental curriculum.

Picasso's Paint Brush

Art instruction that teaches how to fall in love in with painting. We teach people how to bring out their inner artist and how to tell their personal stories through art. Our programs focus on art skill, personal enlightnment and expressive story tellling.

Dexterity

The most enjoyable math and science curriculum you’ve ever experienced. We use hands and tactile games and challenges to teach mathematical and scientific foundations that last a lifetime. We develop strong executive functioning skills for foundational learning.

Page 3 - Trek to Adventures

Adventures in Your Community

Meet up with other homeschoolers interested in adventure, experience and community.

Outward Bound

An Outward Bound program is about bringing out the best of every individual in the outdoors. It creates positive experiences and memories that have a lasting impact.

Sailing for Families

Sailing for Families is a program that combines amateur sailing instruction with life skills. We are committed to building up the next generation of sailors while building up the next generation of families.

Lifeskills through Camping

Lifeskills through camping is a progressive collection of survival skills training. We teach lifeskills in practical and engaging ways. Our programs encourage physical and mental health.
