Activities in Your Community

Never underestimate the learning power of local activities. All local activities can be expericienced with or without supplemental curriculum.

Picasso’s Paint Brush

Art instruction that teaches how to fall in love in with painting. We teach people how to bring out their inner artist and how to tell their personal stories through art. Our programs focus on art skill, personal enlightnment and expressive story tellling.

Dexterity

The most enjoyable math and science curriculum you’ve ever experienced. We use hands and tactile games and challenges to teach mathematical and scientific foundations that last a lifetime. We develop strong executive functioning skills for foundational learning.

Homeschoolers can be Timid

We hope that as you and your homeschooler venture out into your community that you will learn to love many aspects of your community as you participate in the activities and adventures in your neighborhood and as you create opportunities to serve, learn and go with your friends and neighbors
