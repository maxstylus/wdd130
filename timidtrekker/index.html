Your Homeschool is Not at Home

Every homeschooling parent knows that homeschooling has very little to do with schooling at home. We know that the best education does not happen at a desk at all, but rather as we engage in everyday living, in exploring and studying the world around us and as we engage in our communities, develop relationships and experience everyday living.

Ditch the Workbooks

The Timid Trekker works hard for you to collect opportunities for homeschooling parents to teach their children the love of learning. We aim to inspire you to get your child outdoors, learning in your community more and freed from endless workbooks and worksheets.

Come out and Play

Learning should be joyful. We learn most when we are in a state of play. Yet again and again we teach our children in the most tedious and laborious ways possible. All activities can be simply enjoyed or you can use some of our resources to incorporate some other educational topics such math, writing, art and music.
